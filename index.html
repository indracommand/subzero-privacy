Privacy Policy

SubZero — Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Aetryx built SubZero as a commercial app. This page informs visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share personal data. All data you create within the app is stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced privately via your iCloud account.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or tracking pixels. We have no servers that receive your data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health, financial, or biometric data

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services. If the app uses iCloud for syncing, that is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to retain. Data stored on your device or in your iCloud account is under your control. You can delete it at any time by removing the app or clearing its data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, there is no risk of inadvertent collection from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
