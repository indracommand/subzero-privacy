SubZero

Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Overview

SubZero is a subscription tracking app developed by Aetryx. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how SubZero handles your data.

Data Collection

SubZero does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. We have no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising SDKs integrated into the app.

Data Storage

All subscription data you enter into SubZero is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers operated by Aetryx or any third party.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced across your devices via Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Aetryx does not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

SubZero does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or crash reporting SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No social media tracking
  • No data brokers or external data sharing

Children's Privacy

SubZero does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can address the concern.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@aetryx.com.
